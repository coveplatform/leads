Common questions
How does Cove work?

When a customer calls your number and you don't answer, your phone carrier forwards the missed call to Cove. Cove then immediately texts that person back with your qualification questions — one at a time.

When they finish replying, you get a summary sent to your phone or email: their name, what they need, and their number. You call back only the leads worth your time.

How do I set up call forwarding?

Go to Lead sources → Missed calls in the sidebar. Pick your carrier (Telstra, Optus, Vodafone, or other) and follow the steps — it takes about 30 seconds on your phone.

If you're on a desktop right now, you can send the setup code to your mobile from that same page.

What does my customer actually see?

They get a normal-looking text message from your Cove number. The first message introduces your business and asks the first question. It looks like a real person texting — not a robot.

You can see exactly what they see — and edit the messages — under SMS Questions.

How do I test that it's working?

Call your number from a different phone and don't answer — just let it ring out. Within a few seconds, that number should receive your first SMS question from Cove.

If nothing arrives, the most common cause is that call forwarding isn't active yet on your phone. Check Lead sources → Missed calls and make sure you've dialled the setup code.

Can I edit my questions?

Yes — go to SMS Questions, make your changes, and hit Save. Changes go live instantly. There's no minimum number of questions and no limit on edits.

What happens if someone calls after hours?

If you've turned on operating hours under Alerts & Hours, anyone who calls outside those hours gets an after-hours message instead of your full question flow. You can set your hours and choose which days you're closed.

How do I pause or cancel?

Go to Billing in the sidebar. You can pause your account or cancel your subscription at any time — no lock-in, no cancellation fee.

If you cancel, your number stays active until the end of your billing period.

My Cove number isn't texting people — what do I do?

A few things to check first:

  • Call forwarding is active on your phone (test by calling yourself from another number)
  • Your account is active — check the badge next to your business name on the Overview page
  • Your SMS Questions have at least one message saved
