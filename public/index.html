How long does setup take?

Self-serve: most people are live in under 15 minutes. You describe your business, the AI writes your questions, you add your notification number and pay. Done. Done-for-you: live within 48 hours.

Do I need a new phone number?

No. You keep your existing number. Cove gives you a dedicated AU SMS number. A one-time call forwarding setup (2 minutes, we walk you through it) sends missed calls to Cove instead of voicemail.

What does the money-back guarantee cover?

If Cove doesn’t pay for itself within 30 days of going live, contact us and we’ll give you a full refund — including the setup fee if you chose done-for-you. No conditions.

Does it work with my existing website?

Yes. WordPress, Squarespace, Wix, Webflow, custom HTML — anything with a form or a click-to-call button. We hook into whatever you’ve already got.

I already use Jobber / ServiceTitan / Podium — will it clash?

No clash. The moment Cove finishes qualifying a lead, it fires a structured summary — name, number, job type, urgency — to your CRM via webhook, or anywhere you want via Zapier. You stay in the tools you already know.

Can I customise the questions?

Yes. The AI generates them from your description, but you can edit any question directly in your dashboard. Or click “Regenerate” to get a fresh set.

What if the lead doesn’t reply?

Cove sends a gentle follow-up after a set time. If they still don’t respond, you get whatever info was collected so you can follow up yourself. No lead disappears silently.
