How long does setup take?

Self-serve: most people are live in under 15 minutes. Describe your business, review your questions, add your notification number and pay. Done. Done-for-you: live within 48 hours.

Do I need a new phone number?

No. You keep your existing number. Cove gives you a dedicated AU SMS number. A one-time call forwarding setup (2 minutes, we walk you through it) sends missed calls to Cove instead of voicemail.

Does it work with my existing website?

Yes. WordPress, Squarespace, Wix, Webflow, custom HTML — anything with a form or a click-to-call button. We hook into whatever you’ve already got.

I already use Jobber / ServiceTitan / Podium — will it clash?

No clash. The moment Cove finishes qualifying a lead, it fires a structured summary — name, number, job type, urgency — to your CRM via webhook, or anywhere you want via Zapier. Cove qualifies. Your CRM manages. Nothing changes in your workflow.

What does the money-back guarantee cover?

If Cove doesn’t pay for itself within 7 days of going live, contact us and we’ll give you a full refund. No conditions.

Can I customise the questions?

Yes. Your questions come pre-built, but you can edit any of them directly in your dashboard — and changes go live instantly.
